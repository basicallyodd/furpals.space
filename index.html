Server Rules

Please adhere to these rules while in our Discord server.

  1. Please use your fursona(or fursonas) as your name, you can switch your nickname any time.
  2. Please be nice to each other, this is a space for good connections, not drama.
  3. Please use the correct channels.
  4. Please @mods when you need assistance or if someone isn't following the rules!
